International students at celegance 2023


Written By Willy March 2, 2024

Entertainment

C.V Raman Global University recently hosted an enchanting cultural exchange event, "Celegance," which brought together international students from diverse backgrounds to celebrate their unique cultures. The event aimed to foster understanding, appreciation, and friendship among students from various parts of the globe. The vibrant atmosphere of Celegance was palpable as attendees showcased their traditional attire, shared their native cuisines, and performed captivating dances and music from their respective countries. From the rhythmic beats of African drumming to the graceful movements of Asian classical dance, the event was a testament to the rich tapestry of cultures represented at CGU University. Interactive displays allowed participants to delve deeper into each other's customs, languages, and traditions, sparking engaging conversations and forging lasting connections. Students eagerly exchanged stories about their homelands, discussing everything from cultural practices to societal norms, thereby broadening their perspectives and enriching their global awareness.
