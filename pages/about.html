About CGU tabloid

Welcome to the CGU Tabloid News Club! We're a dynamic group of students passionate about storytelling, journalism, and keeping our university community informed and engaged. At CGU, we believe in the power of words to spark conversations, inspire change, and unite our campus. From breaking news to feature stories, opinion pieces to event coverage, our team is dedicated to bringing you the latest happenings and diverse perspectives from across campus. We're not just reporters; we're storytellers, capturing the essence of life at CGU university through our articles, videos, and multimedia projects. Whether it's highlighting student achievements, exploring campus culture, or delving into important issues, we strive to be the voice of our university. But we're more than just a news organization; we're a community. We welcome students from all backgrounds and disciplines to join us in our mission to inform, inspire, and ignite curiosity. Together, we're shaping the narrative of CGU and creating a space where every voice is heard. Join us as we embark on this exciting journey of discovery, learning, and storytelling. Because at CGU Tabloid, every story matters.
